We Are Utsab
The non-profit Bengali cultural organization Utsab thrives on preserving and upholding the deep-seated customs and cultural legacy of Bengali's. This group, which was founded in 2023, will work to provide successful events that build a strong and vibrant Bengali community in the land of the Dutch, distant from the homeland.
